Mucho gusto!

Take a trip with me and find your new favorite place to be!


In the spring of 2017, I was finishing my freshman year of college and one of my advisors mentioned the options I had for my degree to study abroad. I flipped throught the packet she gave me and saw Barcelona and I instantly knew that's where I wanted to go. The program would be a year long and I didn't know anyone else who would be going.

The whole summer I thought about what I would be doing and packed and repacked my bags. To say I was nervous as hell was an understatement.

From the moment I got off the plane and stepped into the beautiful airport, I was hooked. The taxi ride from the airport to the city was simultaneously the most nervewracking and most exciting trip of my life. It was the first time I was really doing something on my own.

During my year living in Barcelona, I fell in love. The city became my second home. I traveled around the country and I knew that my next steps would be doing whatever I could to come back after graduation.

And I did.

Moving back to the place I fell in love with was a dream come true.

Now I help other people fall in love with Spain just like I did!
